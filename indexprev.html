History


Since 2014, Paragon Organization has been enabling youth by providing opportunities for individual growth, through exploring and learning in the information technology field. The Paragon Organization has built a makerspace to give youth members the place, tools and guidance necessary for them to engage with technology. Our organization provides plentiful hands on, youth led activitiies. Activities and programs are inclusive, encourage teamwork and peer interaction. There is also ample opportunity for supervised individual projects, as well.
